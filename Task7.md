### SPECKit Plus
#### üîπ What is SPECKit Plus?

SPECKit Plus is a Spec-Driven Development (SDD) framework designed to replace unstructured "vibe coding" with a disciplined, AI-guided workflow. It uses a series of slash commands to enforce a strict progression from high-level rules to granular code execution, ensuring AI outputs are consistent, maintainable, and aligned with project requirements.
