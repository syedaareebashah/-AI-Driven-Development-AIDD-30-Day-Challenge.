# AIDD 30-Day Challenge — Task 1

#### What does AI-Driven Development mean to you?

AI-Driven Development (AIDD) is a new development style where artificial intelligence tools and agents play a central role in the software development lifecycle. For me, AIDD means working with AI systems that can reason, plan, and even code alongside human developers. Instead of writing every line of code manually, developers now collaborate with AI to co-create solutions, automate repetitive tasks, and focus more on solving problems and strategizing project goals.

#### How do you see the future of Human-AI Collaboration in software development?

I believe the future of Human-AI Collaboration is about partnership, not competition. As AI tools—like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Claude—become more advanced, developers will act more like project managers or designers, guiding AI agents, setting milestones, and evaluating and improving outputs rather than just coding syntax. Human creativity will combine with AI's speed and intelligence, resulting in more innovative and high-quality solutions. Developers will need new skills: prompt engineering, evaluating AI-generated work, and reasoning about project goals. The biggest change is that software development will be about "what to build and why" rather than only "how to code it." The October 2025 Turning Point marked the official shift toward this collaborative era.
